## First Time Setup

### Connecting to WiFi

Your radio needs WiFi to play streaming stations. Here's how to connect:

1. **Plug in the power adapter**
   The radio will start up (this takes about 1 minute)

2. **Look for a WiFi network on your phone**
   - Open WiFi settings on your phone or tablet
   - Look for a network named **"RadioSetup-r1"** (or similar)
   - Connect to this network
   - No password is needed

3. **Setup page opens automatically**
   - Your phone's browser should open automatically to the setup page
   - If it doesn't, open your browser and go to: **http://10.41.0.1**

4. **Select your home WiFi network**
   - You'll see a list of available WiFi networks
   - Tap on your home WiFi network name
   - Enter your WiFi password
   - Tap "Connect"

5. **Wait for connection**
   - The radio will connect to your WiFi (takes about 30 seconds)
   - The "RadioSetup" network will disappear
   - Your radio is now connected!

**Note:** If you need to change WiFi networks later, simply unplug the radio from your current WiFi network and repeat these steps.

## Using Your Radio

### Controls

**Bank Switch (Left Switch)**
- Turn to select a bank of stations (0-9)
- Each bank contains 10 different stations

**Station Switch (Right Switch)**
- Turn to select a station within the current bank (0-9)
- The radio will immediately start playing

**Volume Knob**
- Turn clockwise to increase volume
- Turn counter-clockwise to decrease volume

**Volume Knob Button**
- Press once to pause
- Press again to resume

### Quick Start

1. Turn the **Bank Switch** to position 0 (Jazz stations)
2. Turn the **Station Switch** to position 0
3. Music will start playing automatically
4. Adjust volume using the **Volume Knob**

## Troubleshooting

### No Sound

**Check the volume:**
- Turn the volume knob clockwise
- Try pressing the volume knob button (it might be paused)

**Check if it's playing:**
- The station switches should both be pointing to a number (0-9)
- Try turning one of the switches to a different position

**Check WiFi connection:**
- Banks 0-7 require internet connection
- Banks 8-9 work without internet

### Can't Connect to WiFi

**Try again:**
1. Unplug the radio
2. Wait 10 seconds
3. Plug it back in
4. Wait 1 minute for the "RadioSetup" network to appear
5. Follow the WiFi setup steps again

**Check your WiFi password:**
- Make sure you entered it correctly (it's case-sensitive)
- Check that your WiFi is working on other devices

### RadioSetup Network Doesn't Appear

**Wait longer:**
- It can take up to 2 minutes for the network to appear after plugging in

**Check if already connected:**
- The radio might already be connected to your WiFi
- Try turning the switches - if music plays, you're connected!

### Station Won't Play / Silence

**Try a different station:**
- Some internet stations go offline occasionally
- Turn the station switch to a different number

**Check your internet:**
- Make sure your WiFi is working
- Try loading a website on your phone

**Try Banks 8 or 9:**
- These stations don't require internet
- If these work, your internet connection might be the issue

### Volume is Too Loud/Quiet

**Adjust the minimum volume:**
- The volume knob has a minimum level set to 50%
- This prevents it from being too quiet
- You can adjust this by contacting support

## Care and Maintenance

### Placement
- Keep away from water and moisture
- Place on a stable, flat surface
- Keep air vents clear for cooling

### Cleaning
- Unplug before cleaning
- Wipe with a soft, dry cloth
- Do not use liquid cleaners

### Storage
- If not using for extended periods, unplug from power
- Store in a dry location
